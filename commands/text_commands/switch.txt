Everything about the power switch:
- What is its use? You need to activate it to buy perks, to use ultimate machine or to use team machine (it will also switch on the redstone lamps in power station room in Dead End).
- Price? **1000** gold to activate it.
- Where is it?
    > In Dead End : **Power Station**
    > In Bad Blood : **Balcony**
    **Info**: When you activate the power switch in the Bad Blood map, it will summon **Clarence** (a mini-boss).
- You only need to activate it once for the entire game.
