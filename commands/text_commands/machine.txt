Everything about the team machine:
- Where is it?
    > In Dead End: **Roof Top**
    > In Bad Blood: **Great Hall**
- What is its utility? With the team machine you can buy 3 things (at will):
    > **Ammo Supply** (arrow) -> refill ammos for all players. (**1000** gold)
    > **Full Revive** (golden apple) -> Revives all dead or knocked down teammates. (**2000** gold)
    > **Dragon's Wrath** (dragon egg) -> Kill all mobs in a radius of 15 around the team machine. The price increases of 1,000 per use. (**5000** base gold)
